Directive 2011/83/EU on consumer rights

Article 18 - Delivery
Unless otherwise agreed, trader shall deliver goods by transferring physical possession/control to consumer without undue delay and not later than 30 days from contract conclusion.
If trader fails to fulfil obligation at agreed time or within applicable period, consumer shall call upon trader to make delivery within an additional period appropriate to the circumstances.
If trader fails to deliver within additional period, consumer is entitled to terminate the contract.

Article 13 - Reimbursement by trader
Trader shall reimburse all payments received from consumer without undue delay and in any event not later than 14 days from being informed of consumer's decision to withdraw/terminate where applicable.
