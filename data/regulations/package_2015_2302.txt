Directive (EU) 2015/2302 on package travel and linked travel arrangements

Article 12 - Termination of the package travel contract and right of withdrawal before start of package
Traveller may terminate in specific circumstances; organiser may terminate under defined conditions.
Where contract is terminated under applicable provisions, traveller is entitled to full refund of payments made for package.

Article 13 - Responsibility for performance and remedies
Organiser is responsible for performance of travel services and shall provide remedies where services are not in conformity.

Article 14 - Price reduction and compensation for damages
Traveller may be entitled to price reduction and/or compensation in applicable situations.

Article 12(4) / refund timing principle
Refunds due to traveller after lawful termination are generally to be provided without undue delay and typically within 14 days.
