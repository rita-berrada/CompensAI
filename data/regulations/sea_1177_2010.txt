Regulation (EU) No 1177/2010 concerning rights of passengers when travelling by sea and inland waterway

Article 18 - Re-routing and reimbursement in the event of cancelled or delayed departures
Passengers shall be offered choice of rerouting or reimbursement under defined conditions.

Article 19 - Compensation of ticket price in the event of delay in arrival
Passengers may claim compensation of 25% or 50% of ticket price depending on scheduled journey duration and delay band.

Article 17 - Assistance in event of cancelled or delayed departures
Carrier/terminal operator shall provide assistance where conditions are met.
