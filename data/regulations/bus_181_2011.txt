Regulation (EU) No 181/2011 concerning rights of passengers in bus and coach transport

Article 2 - Scope
Key provisions on rerouting/reimbursement apply to regular services where scheduled distance is 250 km or more.

Article 19 - Continuation, re-routing and reimbursement
When overbooking, cancellation, or delay in departure of more than 120 minutes occurs from a terminal for covered services, carrier shall offer continuation/re-routing or reimbursement.

Article 21 - Assistance in event of cancellation or delayed departure
For long delays/cancellations, carrier may be required to provide assistance.
