Regulation (EU) 2021/782 on rail passengers' rights and obligations

Article 19 - Compensation in respect of ticket price
Without losing the right to transport, passengers may request compensation for delay between place of departure and destination.
Minimum compensation shall be 25% of ticket price for a delay of 60 to 119 minutes.
Minimum compensation shall be 50% of ticket price for a delay of 120 minutes or more.

Article 18 - Reimbursement and re-routing
Where delay is expected to exceed thresholds, passengers may choose reimbursement or continuation/re-routing under conditions set out in this Regulation.

Article 20 - Assistance
In the event of delays, passengers are entitled to information and assistance measures.
